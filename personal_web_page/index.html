About Me

Hello! I am Metehan. I was born in 1998 in Ankara. Currently, I am studying Geomatics Engineering at Hacettepe University. My goal is to find solutions for spatial data included programs.

In my third year in university, I went for Erasmus Program at Politecnico di Milano University in Italy and improved my knowledge and skills about GIS and Photogrammetry subjects.

Also I did my first internship at General Command of Mapping in Turkey. In my Internship I mostly focused on plugin development for GIS programmes and Photogrammetric process for small scaled maps.

Here are a few technologies I've been working with recently:

  • Python(arcpy)
  • Arcgis
  • HTML & CSS
  • QGIS
  • PostgreSQL/Postgis
  • Matlab
